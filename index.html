Inmigration In Finland

✦ About this project

This project was born from two perspectives: as a Data Scientist, and as an Latin American immigrant living in Finland. Both shape the way I approach this topic — but neither defines the data itself.
The goal here is not to take sides. It’s not to support or oppose immigration. It’s simply to present the facts, as they are, in a way that is easy to read, understand, and reflect on.
All the data used comes from official Finnish statistics. These are numbers that already exist — but often remain hidden behind complex reports or hard-to-navigate websites. This platform brings them to light.
What people often want to know is straightforward:
Are immigrants working? Are they integrating? Are they relying on welfare? Are they contributing to society — or causing concern?
These are real questions. And data can help answer them, without drama, without manipulation, and without jumping to conclusions.
This project aims to shed light — not heat. To inform — not persuade.
How that information is used is up to each reader.
